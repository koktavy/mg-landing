MERIT GENERATOR / 01
Core Proposition

“The Merit Generator stages structured deliberation, disagreement, consensus formation, memory, and philosophical output through a visible sculptural form.”

The system transforms AI from an invisible assistant into an observable ritual of reasoning. It creates artifacts of output that become quotes, objects, editions, archives, and site-specific activations.

Core assumption: the strongest presentation is the visible working system first, then the theory. Though the theory activates the form.

A New Category

We present deliberation as medium. It is a physical system where argument, consensus, and output are part of the artwork.

Sculpture

Presence, aura, installation value, documentation value.

Reasoning Engine

Agents deliberate, challenge, revise, and converge.

Memory Archive

Past outputs shape future context and system guidance.

Publishing Machine

Statements become prints, books, objects, archives, and editions.

Deliberative Architecture

Clear Light is the primary synthesizer module. It reads the discussion and consensus after the agents converge, then produces a higher-level synthesis and considers the next deliberation topic.

The art is the cycle:
topic → disagreement →
memory → convergence → artifact.

Merit Studio

Merit Studio is the physical space where Merit Generator lives, is exhibited, worked on, and allowed to evolve.

Working Installation

Not a storage room or passive display. The studio is where the system runs, changes, is observed, repaired, and documented.

Experimental Access

Accessible for testing topics, agent behavior, output formats, material translations, and public-facing installation modes.

Living Archive

The space holds the traces of deliberations: transcripts, printed outputs, prototypes, sculptural units, and future editions.

The studio is not backstage. It is part of the instrument.

Merit Beacon

Merit Beacon is the outward signal of Merit Generator: a translation of deliberative outputs into public light, language, limited artworks, and collective gathering.

As an Output

Merit statements, consensus traces, and system logs become scores for light, plotter prints, 3D prints, projections, and editioned objects.

As an Event

A public activation around a living AI sculpture: opening gathering, live deliberation, artist talks, documentation, and limited releases.

As a Signal

A beacon for merit, not commerce alone: a visible ritual of ethical computation, attention, and shared responsibility.

MERIT BEACON / OUTPUT

Consecrated System
High Tibetan lamas performing a Rabne consecration ritual around Merit Generator.

"Merit Generator is the world's first AI sculptural art project consecrated by high Tibetan lamas."

The lamas performed a Rabne ritual, usually reserved for auspicious and precious objects such as statues, thangkas, or stupas.

The system is not only a technical apparatus. It is a consecrated sculptural object: a machine that generates merit for all. Engine for goodness.

Artist Bios

Merit Generator is created by Mehow Skalski, Ori Carino, and Tavius Koktavy. The project brings together AI systems, contemplative art, spatial media, sculptural technology, and public-facing cultural outputs.

Mehow Skalski

Technology and new media artist working across AI systems, VR/AR, 3D scanning, 360 video, immersive documentation, sculptural technology, systems architecture, and logic. Co-creator of Merit Generator and Merit Beacon.

Ori Carino

NYC-based artist whose work spans painting, installation, ritual imagery, and contemporary Buddhist visual culture. A trained thangka painter and co-creator of Merit Generator and Merit Beacon, with work extending into public-facing outputs.

Tavius Koktavy

Co-creator of Merit Generator and Merit Beacon, augmented and virtual reality expert, and artist working with 3D scanning projects and system logic.
